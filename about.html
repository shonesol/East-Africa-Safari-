About Brendava Tours & Travel

Brendava Tours & Travel is a trusted East African tour company based in Kampala, Uganda, dedicated to creating unforgettable travel experiences across Uganda, Kenya, Tanzania, and Rwanda. We specialize in wildlife safaris, gorilla trekking, birdwatching tours, cultural experiences, adventure travel, and customized holiday packages.

Our experienced guides and professional team are passionate about showcasing the beauty, wildlife, and diverse cultures of East Africa. Whether you dream of meeting mountain gorillas in Bwindi, witnessing the Great Migration in the Serengeti and Maasai Mara, or exploring the magnificent landscapes of East Africa, we are here to make your journey exceptional.
