Uganda's Game Parks & Lakes Map

Uganda, the “Pearl of Africa,” is a beautiful country with diverse scenery shown on its map. It has lush green forests, rolling hills, large lakes like Lake Victoria, and wildlife-filled savannahs. The country is also home to the River Nile and stunning national parks such as Bwindi and Murchison Falls. Its mix of mountains, water, and wildlife makes it one of the most scenic destinations in Africa
