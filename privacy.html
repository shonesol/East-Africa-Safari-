Brendava Tours & Travel values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information obtained through our website, booking forms, emails, telephone communications, and other interactions with our company.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed toward children under the age of 13 without parental or guardian supervision. We do not knowingly collect information from children without appropriate consent.

Depending on applicable laws, you may have the right to request access to your personal information, request corrections, request deletion of certain information, or withdraw consent for specific uses.
